Chocolate Ganache Icing

Description

This frosting recipe quickly makes a rich, creamy, and dark chocolate icing with just bittersweet chocolate, brandy, butter, and heavy cream.

Ingredients

  • 7 (1 ounce) squares bittersweet chocolate
  • Â½ cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons brandy

Directions

  1. Melt chocolate, cream, butter or margarine, and brandy together in a high-sided saucepan over medium heat, stirring frequently, until smooth.
  2. Cool completely and then spread or pipe over cake or cupcakes.
